🚀 Quick Start: Get Your Live URL in 5 Minutes!

Step 1: Create GitHub Repository

1. Go to github.com and sign in

2. Click "New repository"

3. Name it: math-shooter-tiktok

4. Make it Public and click "Create repository"

Step 2: Upload Files

Upload all files from your GitHub_Package folder:

index.html
README.md
LICENSE
docs/SETUP_GUIDE.md
docs/VIRAL_TIPS.md

Step 3: Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to Settings → Pages

2. Select "Deploy from a branch" → "main" → "/ (root)"

3. Click Save

Step 4: Get Your URL!

https://yourusername.github.io/math-shooter-tiktok/

Your game will be live in 5-10 minutes!
